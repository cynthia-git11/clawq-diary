Q2 · Historical IRR / MOIC median?

Prior 7 funds returned $550M+ in cash to LPs. Per-fund IRR / MOIC / DPI / TVPI by vintage walked through 1-on-1 with IR after NDA — audited only.

Q3 · Key Person clauses?

Cynthia is named Key Person. Departure triggers an LP vote to suspend the investment period; second-tier partners have all followed 4+ prior funds — bios after NDA.

Q4 · LP confidentiality?

LP roster never published. Annual LPACs name no individual; quarterly reports are anonymized by account ID. Passed 4 sovereign-fund ODD audits.

Q5 · Co-invest opportunities?

$10M+ tickets get auto pro-rata co-invest · zero mgmt fee · zero carry. 12 standalone co-invests created across our prior 6 funds.

Q6 · Reporting cadence?

Quarterly LP reports + monthly emails + daily public diary + annual on-site LPAC. Reports include vintage cash flow timelines, mark deltas, exit forecasts, risk disclosures.

Q7 · Mgmt fee & carry?

Standard 2% / 20%, 8% hurdle full-stack, European waterfall. $25M+ tickets negotiate bespoke fee discount / MFN after NDA.

Q8 · Investment period & lifespan?

Investment period 3 years (extendable 1y); fund life 10 years (extendable 2×1y). For the AI Agent window, ~70% deploy in first 18 months.

Q9 · Risk factors?

(1) AI cost deflation outpacing app monetization; (2) US-China Agent ecosystem decoupling; (3) hyperscaler free tiers crushing tooling. Hedge: end-to-end + hardware + enterprise security.

Q10 · Why trust us?
